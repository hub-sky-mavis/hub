Cookie Policy for hub.skymavis.com

Last Updated: March 19, 2025

1. Introduction

This Cookie Policy explains how hub.skymavis.com uses cookies and similar tracking technologies when you visit our website about Decentralized Autonomous Organizations (DAOs) in game governance.

2. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device that help us improve your browsing experience, analyze site traffic, and personalize content. They do not contain personal identifying information directly but can help us recognize your browser and preferences.

3. Types of Cookies We Use

3.1 Essential Cookies

  • Purpose: Enable core website functionality
  • Examples:
    • Authentication tokens
    • Session management
    • Security protection
  • Retention: Typically 24 hours to 1 year

3.2 Analytics Cookies

  • Purpose: Understand website usage and performance
  • Tools Used:
    • Google Analytics
    • Matomo
  • Data Collected:
    • Page views
    • User interactions
    • Device information
  • Retention: Up to 2 years

3.3 Marketing Cookies

  • Purpose: Personalize advertising and track marketing effectiveness
  • Examples:
    • Conversion tracking
    • Audience segmentation
  • Third-Party Providers:
    • Google Ads
    • Facebook Pixel
  • Retention: Up to 1 year

3.4 Preference Cookies

  • Purpose: Remember user preferences and settings
  • Examples:
    • Language selection
    • Dark/light mode
  • Retention: Up to 1 year

4. User Consent and Management

4.1 Consent Mechanism

  • Explicit opt-in required before non-essential cookies are set
  • Granular consent options for different cookie categories
  • Easy withdrawal of consent at any time

4.2 Cookie Management Instructions

  1. Use browser settings to manage cookies
  2. Visit our cookie preferences center
  3. Adjust settings for each cookie category

4.3 Browser Settings

  • Chrome: Settings > Privacy > Cookies
  • Firefox: Options > Privacy & Security
  • Safari: Preferences > Privacy
  • Edge: Settings > Cookies and site permissions

5. Third-Party Cookies

We use third-party services that may place cookies on your device:

  • Analytics platforms
  • Advertising networks
  • Social media integrators

6. User Rights Under GDPR

As a user, you have the right to:

  • Access your data
  • Request cookie deletion
  • Withdraw consent
  • Object to data processing
